Inception

I like to be known as a Creative Technologist

I am an Arduino Maker Fellow and was mentored by the likes of Ankit Daftery and Avik Dhupar from Team Arduino-India. I am a graduate from Bangalore Institute Of Technnology, class of 2014, Bangalore, India. I believe that creativity is the result of the constraints one has to work around. I am a debater, foodie, musician , traveller and a photographer at Rishi Bhatnagar Photography I work at Target as a Trainee Developer.

Research and Projects

I have conducted various events and workshops , I like brainstorming , prototyping and making things that have a potential to solve a problem , no matter how small it may be. I like creating and enabling people to create things. I also like making my way around software, it is all a part of an ever learning process.
